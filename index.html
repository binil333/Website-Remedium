What is Remedium?

Remedium is a free online tool to help people identify the health of their Kidney. It predicts whether an individual has early signs of Chronic Kidney Disease(CKD) or might develop CKD in future. It uses Machine Learning and scientific data to analyse the current conditions of the individual and provides a applicable result.

How dangerous is Chronic Kidney Disease?

Chronic kidney disease, also called chronic kidney failure, involves a gradual loss of kidney function. Your kidneys filter wastes and excess fluids from your blood, which are then removed in your urine. Advanced chronic kidney disease can cause dangerous levels of fluid, electrolytes and wastes to build up in your body.
